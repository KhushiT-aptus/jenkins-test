Backend Design & Architecture Document
Project Name: Multi-Organization AI Chatbot Platform
Integration Type: Embedded chatbot in third-party services
Architecture Type: Microservices with Multi-Tenant Support

1. System Overview
The AI chatbot management system is designed to serve multiple organizations with isolated configurations, role-based access control (RBAC), and department-level knowledge bases. The chatbot can be embedded into existing third-party applications, while a dashboard UI supports administrative tasks such as user management, organization creation, chatbot configuration & management and analytics.
